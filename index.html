About

James Johnson (M.Ed. / University of Minnesota) has been a teacher and web developer for the better part of two decades. Currently, he's the Director of Online Learning at the Minneapolis College of Art and Design, where he works with amazing faculty to create engaging learning experiences online.

In his creative work, James explores the intersection of design and mathematics through code.

James is a proud Minnesoohhtan, and lives in the Minneapolis area with his wonderful hubby and two destructive cats.

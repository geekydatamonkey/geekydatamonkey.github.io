About

Hello there!

I'm James. I'm a teacher, developer, and data geek at the Minneapolis College of Art and Design.

I have my Master's Degree in Education from the University of Minnesota, and have taught both online and face-to-face classes since the early 2000's. Mostly, I teach web development now... which is amazing and fun and feels a bit like teaching magic!
